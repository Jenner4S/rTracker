Replace rTrackerA Now.

  • rTrackerA, the advertising supported, in-app upgrade version of rTracker, is being discontinued.


  • Please install rTracker, which is now free on the App Store and open source.


  • You can easily transfer your trackers with data to rTracker:

    • Go into your tracker in rTrackerA
    • From the menu in the top right corner, select "email Tracker+Data"
    • Send the tracker to yourself

    • In your iOS mail program, find the email with your tracker (it may be in your 'Sent Mail' folder)
    • Tap the attachement, and select 'Copy to rTracker' from the list (you may have to scroll the list of apps)

    • When rTracker appears with your tracker, tap the green 'Accept' button in the top right.

  • More information on importing is here.
